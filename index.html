Who are we?

SMILE AND SAY PAPADI

21 Chunks is a supplementary Edutainment programme providing Play-centred learning activities, Broadcasted educational programmes and Tutoring services. We provide support to Schools, Teachers, Parents and Home-based learning. Our drive is to assist the wholistic development of Children. We propel the process of building the next generation of Greats by providing fun “Learn through Play” programmes.

At 21 Chunks you find: Puppet shows, Games (Modern and Indigenous) , Music and movement, Crafts and many more. Our activities are always supplementary in nature and because of this, all content and activities are fully based on your preferred Curriculum/Programme.

Helping schools and parents mould future leaders is the wind beneath our wings. We ensure the very best for our littles. Safety is always at the forefront and a fully consultative process with guardians is one of our top priorities. A Fun environment is ensured throughout all our operations.

Little Greats Learn

Welcome to our innovative virtual tutoring service, where learning knows no bounds! We pride ourselves on offering personalized, one-on-one tutoring sessions that transcend geographical limitations, connecting kiddies with expert tutors based on their grade. Through cutting-edge virtual platforms, kids can access high-quality instruction in subjects ranging from mathematics to language development and test preparation, all from the comfort of their own homes. Our dedicated tutors utilize interactive tools, multimedia resources, and tailored lesson plans to cater to each child's unique learning style and academic goals. With flexible scheduling options and convenient access to top-tier facilitators, our virtual tutoring service empowers kids to excel academically and unlock their full potential, no matter where they are.
